## 🎨 VISUAL DESCRIPTION

Imagine this in your header:

```
     ⚡
    /|\     WORKED IN
   / | \    ^^^^^^ ^^
  /  |  \   small  BIG+GLOW
 
Purple  Amber
 side    side
   \  |  /
    \ | /
     \|/
      ⚡ spark
```

**The Mark**:
- Lightning bolt pointing down (energy flowing)
- Left half: Purple gradient (freelancer)
- Right half: Amber gradient (client) with electric glow
- White spark at center where they meet
- Subtle W hint in the middle
- Small electric particles floating around
- Vertical emphasis (taller than wide)

**The Typography**:
- "WORKED" = uppercase, bold (700), neutral color, smaller
- "IN" = uppercase, black (900), 35% BIGGER, gradient purple→amber
- "IN" has electric glow effect
- "IN" positioned slightly higher for emphasis
- Tight letter spacing for modern feel
