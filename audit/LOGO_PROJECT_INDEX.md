## 🎨 THE 6 LOGO CONCEPT IDEAS

If you want inspiration RIGHT NOW without reading the full brief:

### 1️⃣ **Handshake/Connection Mark**
Two abstract shapes meeting where they connect, showing partnership. Purple meets Gold at connection point.

### 2️⃣ **Verification Shield**
Shield or badge shape with checkmark inside. Represents the CIN verification system that makes Khedma unique. Professional, secure feeling.

### 3️⃣ **Growth/Opportunity Arrow**
Upward-pointing arrow showing career progress and income potential. Can be abstract or stylized.

### 4️⃣ **Network/Community Mark**
Interconnected hexagons or circles showing the ecosystem of connected freelancers and clients.

### 5️⃣ **Local + Global**
Tunisia subtly incorporated into a professional mark. Shows local market specialization with global ambition.

### 6️⃣ **Skill/Rating System**
Stars or ascending levels representing portfolio, expertise, and professional ratings.
