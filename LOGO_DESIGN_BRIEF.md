## 🎯 PLATFORM OVERVIEW

### What is Khedma TN?

Khedma TN is a **two-sided B2B freelance marketplace platform** specialized for the Tunisian market. "Khedma" (خدمة) translates to "service" in Arabic, directly expressing the platform's core value proposition.

The platform operates as:
- **For Freelancers:** "Drop the amateurs" - A professional network to showcase skills and earn from quality projects
- **For Clients:** "Work with the elite" - Access to verified, skilled talent for project-based work
- **For Tunisia:** The first modern, localized freelancing platform with Arabic/French support, Tunisian payment integration, and CIN verification

### Core Functionality
1. **Job Marketplace** - Browse, post, and apply to freelance work
2. **Talent Discovery** - AI-powered matching between clients and freelancers
3. **Identity Verification** - CIN-based trust system (unique differentiator)
4. **Wallet & Payments** - Escrow system with Flouci (Tunisian payment method) integration
5. **Contract Management** - Formal project agreements with dispute resolution
6. **Real-time Messaging** - Direct communication platform
7. **Portfolio & Ratings** - Professional profile showcase with earnings tracking

## 🏆 LOGO DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Functional Requirements

1. **Size Versatility**
   - Large format (horizontal banner/website hero): 800x300px
   - Standard logo: 512x512px
   - Small format (favicon): 32x32px, 64x64px
   - Works at 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 96px

2. **Format Requirements**
   - Primary: SVG (scalable, web-optimized)
   - Variants: PNG with transparency, favicon.ico
   - Color: Full color version, single-color (white/dark) versions
   - Responsive: Adjust mark/wordmark ratio based on space

3. **Language Support**
   - English wordmark: "Khedma TN" or "Khedma"
   - Option A: Bilingual (Arabic script + Latin)
   - Option B: Clean mark-only (language-neutral)
   - Must read well in all display contexts

4. **Technical Requirements**
   - File Format: SVG, PNG
   - Color Profile: sRGB
   - Resolution: Min 1200x1200px for PNG
   - Font: System safe or embedded/outlined
   - Scalability: Clear visibility from 16px to 2000px

### Design Specifications

**Logo Type:** Mark + Wordmark (Combination Logo)
- **Mark (Icon):** Symbolic representation (see concepts below)
- **Wordmark:** Professional typography of "Khedma TN"
- **Lockup:** Horizontal (mark left, text right) and stacked (mark top, text below) versions

**Color Variations Required:**
1. Full Color (Primary Purple + Gold accents)
2. Monochrome/Black (single color)
3. Monochrome/White (for dark backgrounds)
4. Dark Mode Variant (optimized for dark UI)
5. Color with White Version (on colored backgrounds)

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Mark-only: 1:1 (square)
- Mark + Wordmark (Horizontal): 4.5:1 (preferred for web)
- Mark + Wordmark (Stacked): 2.5:3 (for app icons, mobile)

## 🎯 DESIGN DIRECTION & REQUIREMENTS

### Style Preferences

**DO Incorporate:**
- ✅ Modern, geometric elements (clean lines, balanced proportions)
- ✅ Professional appearance (suitable for corporate partnerships)
- ✅ Approachable warmth (not cold or corporate)
- ✅ Scalability (looks good at 16px and 1000px)
- ✅ Cultural sensitivity (respects both Arabic and Western design)
- ✅ Color psychology (trust + opportunity)
- ✅ Movement/dynamism (suggests growth, action)
- ✅ Unique differentiation (more than just generic marketplace)

**AVOID:**
- ❌ Over-complicated designs (hard to scale down)
- ❌ Generic marketplace clichés (handshake overused)
- ❌ Culture-specific imagery that alienates other audiences
- ❌ Too trendy (will age poorly)
- ❌ Poor legibility at small sizes
- ❌ Cluttered compositions
- ❌ Colors that don't reflect the brand (purple + gold are locked)

### Design Personality
- **Modern Yet Timeless** - Not trendy, will be relevant for 5+ years
- **Professional But Friendly** - Approachable, not corporate-cold
- **Bold Yet Balanced** - Strong mark, not aggressive
- **Simple Yet Meaningful** - Deep symbolism, clean execution
- **Global Yet Local** - Universal design language with Tunisian identity

### Uniqueness Factor
Must visually communicate:
1. **Verification/Trust** - The CIN identity verification differentiator
2. **Opportunity** - Growth, earning potential for freelancers
3. **Community** - Two-sided marketplace nature
4. **Local Pride** - Tunisian market focus (without being too literal)
